Functional Architecture

The functional architecture outlines the core logical components and their interactions within the Twinova system. It defines how data is acquired from the hardware, processed by the control logic in Sysmac Studio, and synchronized with the virtual environment in DELMIA, ensuring a seamless digital twin experience.
