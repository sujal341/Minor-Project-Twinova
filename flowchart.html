System Flowchart

Control Logic Flow

The system flowchart defines the sequential operations of the multi-robot assembly line. It covers the detection of workpieces, the coordination between the robot arm and the conveyor, and the error-handling routines programmed into the PLC.

Logical Flowchart

System Logic: The Twinova system logic is governed by a robust sequential control algorithm. It begins with a system-wide initialization check, ensuring that all robotic actuators and the conveyor drive are in their 'Home' positions. Upon workpiece detection via ultrasonic sensors, the SCARA substation executes a precision pick-and-place operation, while the Viper 650 handles secondary assembly tasks. Real-time feedback from the physical line is continuously mirrored in the digital twin, allowing for instantaneous performance monitoring and predictive logic adjustments.
Primary Loop

Continuous monitoring of sensor inputs and execution of motion commands for the robotic stations.

Safety Interlocks

Integrated logic to halt operations in case of emergency stop triggers or component misalignment.
