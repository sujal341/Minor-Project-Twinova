Review Paper

What is a Review Paper?

A review paper is a comprehensive summary and critical evaluation of existing research on a specific topic. Unlike original research papers that present new data, a review paper synthesizes findings from multiple scholarly sources to identify trends, gaps, and areas for future investigation. It provides a structured overview of the current state of knowledge, helping readers understand complex subjects without reading every individual study. It serves as a foundational resource for researchers to build upon established theories and methodologies.

Review Paper Overview

Our review paper provides a systematic analysis of the Digital Twin landscape within smart manufacturing. We evaluate the effectiveness of various communication protocols, with a specific focus on OPC UA, for synchronizing industrial robots like the Viper 650 and SCARA with virtual environments. The paper synthesizes technical requirements for low-latency data exchange and proposes a standardized architecture for integrating physical assembly line substations into a unified digital twin framework, ensuring high-fidelity monitoring and control.
